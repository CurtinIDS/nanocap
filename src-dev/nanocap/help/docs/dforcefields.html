For a capped nanotube however, there is restriction to the points included in the force field calculation. A cutoff length is introduced along the nanotube beyond which points are exclude from the force evaluation. This is required to ensure a uniform arrangement of points in the capped region and reduce the concentration of points in the apex of the cap. This cutoff length is automatically determined basen upon the density of points in the nanotube but can be set manually in the options described in Section 2.3
